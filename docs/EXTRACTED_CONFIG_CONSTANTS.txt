19. FAIRNESS RULES
FR-1: >=1 match | FR-2: <=3 same | FR-3: avg>=50% | FR-4: HP<=2.5x | FR-5: >=2 colors
SR-1: +/-15% colors +/-25% tolerance | SR-2: 1 dmg5+ per 20s, 30s tolerance
SR-3: 1 carry-over per 45s, 60s tolerance | SR-4: 1 depth per column, 2 tolerance

20. DEPTH BAIT
Every 3-5th shooter: top low/mismatch, second high/match
Constraint: 2+ of 4 top shooters productive

23. SEED SYSTEM
seed = hash(level_id, attempt_number)
Controls: color assignments, HP variance, speed variance, shooter color, 
damage rolls, depth bait, CRISIS rolls
Does NOT control: fairness overrides, SDR, phases, player

24. LEVEL STRUCTURE
Per world: 20 standard + 2-3 hard + 1 boss + 2 special = ~25 levels
5 worlds = ~125 total

25. BALANCE TARGETS
