Traffic Bomb — Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Traffic Bomb is developed by Nadav W. This privacy policy explains what data is collected when you play Traffic Bomb and how it is used.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or any information that directly identifies you as a person.

The following data is collected automatically:

Advertising Data (via Google AdMob)
Traffic Bomb displays ads provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and use your device's advertising ID to show you relevant ads. AdMob's privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Analytics Data (via Firebase Analytics)
We use Firebase Analytics to understand how players engage with the game. This data is anonymous and aggregated and is used only to improve the game. Firebase's privacy policy: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To show you ads that support the free game
  • To understand gameplay patterns and improve the game
  • We do not sell your data to third parties
  • We do not use your data for any purpose other than those listed above

3. Children's Privacy

Traffic Bomb is rated for Everyone and is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

4. Data Retention

Analytics data is retained by Firebase according to Google's standard retention policies. You can reset or opt out of personalized ads at any time in your device's privacy settings.

5. Your Rights

You can opt out of personalized advertising at any time:
Android: Settings → Privacy → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization

6. Third-Party Services

Traffic Bomb uses the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob: admob.google.com
  • Google Firebase Analytics: firebase.google.com

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted at this URL. Continued use of the game after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
